A Letter to the Soul Behind My Smile

✨ I am okay ✨

🌹 ～ 🖤 ～ 🌹

There are seasons when the darkness seeps in through the cracks —
when the weight of unseen things pulls you under
before you even realize you're drowning. 🌑

But hear this, tender soul:
you are sheltered. You are fed. You are clothed and washed clean.
You are held — maybe even fiercely loved. 🏠🕊️

And still, the mind wanders to the shadows
instead of turning toward the light that floods your days...

💀 ～ ❤️ ～ 💀

Awaken, love. Rise before those shadows
devour the very things that make your chest rise and fall
with something worth waking up for. 🔥

When you are near her — do you feel it?
That rush, that bliss, that sacred flutter
that no word in any language has ever earned the right to name? 💫

You are not wicked. You are not broken. 🖤
You are simply someone who has let wolves
sleep too close to the heart.

🌹 ～ 💔 ～ 🌹

You love her. 💗
Even if the words still live trapped behind your teeth —
you love her.

You go weak at the knees
at the simple miracle of her smile. 😍
Even in the moments you'd rather be anywhere else —
your heartbeat quickens like it knows something you're still learning.

You would move mountains for her. 🏔️
Rearrange the stars. Surrender things
you didn't even know you held sacred —
and she doesn't yet know the full depth of that devotion.

She is the ground you would worship. 🌺
She is beautiful, yes — but more than that,
she is kind. She is good. She is the rare kind of soul
the universe does not make twice. ✨
