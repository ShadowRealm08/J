✦   I am okay   ✦   I am okay   ✦   I am okay   ✦

There are seasons when the dark bleeds in without invitation —
slipping through the cracks of even the most guarded hearts.
You are not broken for feeling it.
You are only human, and beautifully so.

🏠   You are sheltered. You are fed. You are clothed and made clean.
You are cared for — perhaps more deeply than you yet allow yourself to believe.
The light is already here. Let your eyes find it.

Do not let the grey clouds own you, love. 🌑
Do not let the small worries devour
what blooms so brilliantly when you are near her.

Because when you are with her — 💫
oh, when you are with her —
something in you remembers what it means to be alive.
A bliss that no shadow can quite reach.

You are not unkind. You are not lost beyond returning. 🖤
You are simply someone who has let wolves
sleep too near the heart.
It is not too late to change that.

🌹   🖤   ❤️   🖤   🌹

You love her. 💗

Even if the words still rest unspoken behind your teeth —
even if she doesn't yet know the weight of what you carry for her —
you love her. The truth of it lives in your knees
going weak at the mercy of her smile. 😍

Even in the ordinary moments, the unremarkable ones — 🌺
your heart finds its way back to that quick, tender flutter.
It knows what you sometimes forget:
she is worth every sacrifice you have quietly made.

She is beautiful. She is kind. She is rare. ✨
The kind of soul the universe does not draft twice.
The ground beneath her feet is sacred ground.
And you see it — even when the words escape you.

So be tender with yourself, too. 🕊️
Guard what you have. Lean into what is good.
Focus on the warmth, not the cold.
Focus on her — and let that be enough to bring you home.

❤️
